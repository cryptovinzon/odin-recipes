Lasagna

Description

Lasagna made from the finest pasta, freshest tomatoes, and best cheeses from Italy

Don't miss out!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Cook ground beef in hot pan
  3. Assemble pasta, cheese, and ground beef
  4. Bake in oven at 400F for 25 minutes
